Topics

Released To Run

Based on Heb 12:1-2, Released to Run is designed to help women find their unique callings in the body of Christ and run the race marked out for them.

Finding Fearlessness

Based on Phil. 4:6-8, Finding Fearlessness is designed to show women practical ways to combat the disease of this age: anxiety.

Jars of Clay

Based on II Cor. 4:7-9, we take a journey with Paul to find peace in our imperfections. 3-4 sessions can accommodate this material.

Intimacy with God

Based on Mark 12:30-31, we will take the teaching of Jesus and explore what it looks like for the everyday woman to have a love affair with God that is an ecstatic sort of relationship that makes the most rapturous relationship between a man and a women seem like milk and water. CS Lewis
