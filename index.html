About Me

I was raised in the military by a family, escaping an impoverished lifestyle, and not following Jesus. I met Jesus at 11, however my life began to look like the world around me later on. At 22, there was an incident that changed my life forever, and drew me back to Jesus, and I often speak about it. I then began the adventure of discipleship for over 30 years. I married the best man ever 32 years ago, and now with 3 perfectly imperfect children who are now adults! For 5 years I have been speaking at events, leading the church’s Woman’s ministry, and have been the Director of Discipleship Ministries.

Topics

Released To Run

Based on Heb 12:1-2, Released to Run is designed to help women find their unique callings in the body of Christ and run the race marked out for them.

Finding Fearlessness

Based on Phil. 4:6-8, Finding Fearlessness is designed to show women practical ways to combat the disease of this age: anxiety.

Jars of Clay

Based on II Cor. 4:7-9, we take a journey with Paul to find peace in our imperfections. 3-4 sessions can accommodate this material.

Intimacy with God

Based on Mark 12:30-31, we will take the teaching of Jesus and explore what it looks like for the everyday woman to have a love affair with God that is an ecstatic sort of relationship that makes the most rapturous relationship between a man and a women seem like milk and water. CS Lewis

Lets Have a Cup of Coffee

I would love to have a cup of coffee together and discuss if my ministry is right for you. We can meet in person, or virtually. I look forward to hearing from you!
