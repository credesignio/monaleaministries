About Me

What I do

Having served in Woman’s ministries in the past, I am living my best life as the Director of Discipleship Ministries at the church I have attended for the past 22 years!

I also have recently taken up painting! But at the end of the day, my passion is reading, studying and sharing God’s Word and how to apply it to one’s life with inspiring stories, real-life examples, much laughter and occasionally some tears.  Teaching, speaking, leading and evangelism bring life to my bones.
