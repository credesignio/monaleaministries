About Me

I have been married to a lovely man named Gary for 32 years! Gary and I enjoy riding our bikes together along with skiing in the winter and traveling when possible. We continue to tell people that marriage gets better with time and effort!

My Family

Christian is out oldest son, a 26-year-old young man who is sensitive, caring, creative and intelligent. A graduate from Whitworth University, he is working at Schweitzer Engineering with 3D printers.

Out twins, Zach and Maddie are graduates from the University of Idaho. Zach's degree is in film and theater and Maddie's is in medical science. They are both hilarious, fun, loving, hard working and creative.

What I do

Having served in Woman’s ministries in the past, I am living my best life as the Director of Discipleship Ministries at Cross Point Church where we have attended for the past 22 years!

I enjoy panting and having coffee and deep conversation with friends. At the end of the day, my passion is reading, studying and sharing God’s Word while teaching how to apply it to one’s life with inspiring stories, much laughter and occasional tears. I have found pure joy through following the call to teach, speak and lead.
